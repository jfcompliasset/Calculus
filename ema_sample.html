Exponential Moving Averages (EMA)

The Exponential Moving Average (EMA) is a type of moving average that gives more weight to recent data points, making it more responsive to new information compared to a Simple Moving Average (SMA).

EMA Formula:
EMAtoday = (Valuetoday × (Smoothing / (1 + Days))) + (EMAyesterday × (1 - (Smoothing / (1 + Days))))
